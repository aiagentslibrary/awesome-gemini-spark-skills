# ⏰ Gemini Spark Schedule Ideas

## Daily

### 🌅 Morning Command Brief
Every weekday at 7:00 AM, review my calendar, unread priority emails, open tasks, and recent Drive activity. Give me a concise briefing with top priorities, blockers, and suggested time blocks. Do not create or move calendar events without approval.

### 📬 Inbox Priority Scan
Every weekday at 11:30 AM, scan my inbox for urgent messages, unanswered client emails, deadlines, bills, and scheduling requests. Draft suggested replies when useful. Do not send anything without approval.

## Weekly

### 📈 Weekly CEO Dashboard
Every Monday at 8:00 AM, summarize last week's key emails, meetings, tasks, documents, deadlines, and follow-ups. Create a prioritized weekly plan. Ask before creating calendar blocks or editing files.

### 📣 Weekly Content Engine
Every Friday at 10:00 AM, review my recent notes, saved ideas, YouTube research, and content docs. Suggest next week's content themes, hooks, post ideas, and a draft calendar. Ask before creating or editing any public content.

### 🧾 Receipt + Invoice Cleanup
Every Friday at 3:00 PM, find receipts, invoices, renewal notices, and payment confirmations in Gmail and Drive. Summarize them and propose rows for my finance tracker. Ask before editing Sheets or moving files.
